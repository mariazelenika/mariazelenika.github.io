ABOUT ME

"The secret, Alice, is to surround yourself with people who make your heart smile. It's then, only then, that you'll find Wonderland." - Lewis Carroll

Hello, I’m Maria!
I recently graduated with an M.Sc. in Interactive Media Technology from KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm, Sweden.
My academic journey started with a bachelor’s degree in Multimedia Sciences and Technologies from the University of Udine in Pordenone, Italy, where I graduated with 110 cum laude. My bachelor’s thesis focused on "Deep Learning of Automatic Segmentation of COVID-19 Lesions", while my master’s project explored "Weight Perception in Virtual Reality: Combining Velocity Limiting and Control/Display Ratio with Hand Tracking". This work reflects my passion for immersive technologies and their potential to enhance user experience.
I thrive in multicultural environments and excel at teamwork. Through my academic and professional experiences, I have developed strong skills in UX research, including both quantitative and qualitative methods, and I have a keen eye for design, utilizing tools such as Adobe Suite, Blender, and Figma. I’m also proficient in programming languages like Python and C++, as well as game development with Unity, VR, and hand tracking.
Outside of work, I enjoy reading books and manga, cooking for friends, learning new languages, listening to music, watching movies, series, and anime, swimming, and drawing.
Thank you for taking the time to get to know me. I’m excited to see what opportunities the future holds!
