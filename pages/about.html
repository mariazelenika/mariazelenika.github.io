Hello, I am Maria 😊
A student at KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm, Sweden. I am currently pursuing a two-year master's degree in Interactive Media Technology, building on the foundation of my three-year bachelor's degree in Multimedia Sciences and Technologies, which I completed at the University of Udine in Pordenone, Italy.
One of my proudest accomplishments was when my animation "1984" was featured in the "Premio Sergio Amidei 2022" manifestation in Gorizia, Italy. Additionally, I have graduated with 110 cum laude and my thesis focused on the "Deep Learning of automatic segmentation of COVID-19 lesions".
I am a team player and adaptable to multicultural environments and different groups. When I'm not studying or working on my projects, you can find me indulging in my hobbies, such as reading books and manga 📚, cooking for my friends 🍰, studying new languages 🗨, listening to music 🎧, watching movies, series and anime 🎬, swimming 🏊, and drawing 🎨.
Thank you for taking the time to get to know me, and I'm excited to see what opportunities the future holds!
