Spliced

Project description

In the land of Celestial Shore, Aelia, a human fighter, and Ash, a fox spirit, accidentally merge into one body during a heated argument over the Amulet of Souls, a powerful artifact that controls souls and prevents chaos in their world. When Aelia and Ash merged into one body, the Amulet of Souls shattered into pieces scattered throughout Celestial Shore, and hidden within perilous dungeons. To restore the Amulet of Souls and regain their individual forms, Aelia and Ash must learn to trust each other and work together to overcome challenges that test their abilities and their bond.
Can Aelia and Ash successfully retrieve all the fragments of the amulet and separate from each other before it's too late? Or will they remain forever fused as one being?

Toon-shading - Perception of glossiness

Project description

This project came about during a master's course. It explores the perceived glossiness of different materials rendered with a toon-shader on regular and irregular 3D objects. Through an online survey, it was found that participants perceived the gloss on irregular objects as more glossy than on regular objects. This study provides insights into the relationship between material properties, object geometry, and perceived glossiness in toon-shading.

On the Same Page

Project description

This project came about during a master's course. On the Same Page visualizes 1000s of books for book-lovers, who may be struggling to find their next book to read. You can choose the axis of the scatterplot to see the relationship between different book attributes. By simply clicking on a circle you can find detailed information about the book, such as title, author, page count, ratings, awards and more. You can also go to the Goodreads website where you can see what other readers think of the book, and find other related books you might find interest in.

DogGo

Project description

This project came about during a master's course. We used the Double Diamond Method, which has four phases, as can be seen above. First, we brainstormed to understand the design brief, which was about introverts and extroverts in a world affected by COVID. We chose pet owners as our target group and interviewed seven of them to understand their needs. We used the Affinity Diagram method to analyze their responses and define our problem. In the Develop/Ideate phase, we came up with the idea of an app called DogGo, whose main feature would be a map, which you can use to either avoid or meet other pet owners (in this case, dog owners) or attend different events by changing your status in the app. You can also make friends and organize public or private events for your dog and yourself. We made rough sketches and a lo-fi design and got user feedback through a workshop. In the Deliver/Iterated Prototype phase, we used Figma to make a hi-fi design for the app. Overall, it was an interesting experience, and I learned a lot about the design process.

Simed - A Chef's Story

Project description

A young student, Simed Sillab, gets lost while hiking in the mountains. In his aimless wandering, he comes across a cave in which he finds a kind of altar with a cake on it. Tired and hungry, he eats it without thinking twice. Immediately, an earthquake hits the mountain, and a thundering voice cries out in horror at the boy's gesture. Ovres appears and reveals himself to be the servant of the Spirit of the mountain, Oflodor, and explains to Simed that what he ate was indeed his lord's sacred cake. Consuming it has unleashed his anger, and now he has only one way to appease him: to make the cake again. But it won't be that easy because he will have to find the five fundamental ingredients —objects that are anything but normal and easy to recover. Simed has to go through five difficult tests before he finally manages to recover the ingredients and complete the cake.

1984

Project description

The inception of this project can be traced back to the profound impact that George Orwell's "1984" had on me while having to read it during one of my bachelors courses. The spine-chilling terror that coursed through my veins while reading the book served as the impetus behind this endeavor. My goal was to create a visceral experience that would allow others to understand the unrelenting sense of paranoia that Winston, the protagonist, would have felt while being perpetually surveilled by the ominous presence of Big Brother. The piece was also featured at the "Premio Sergio Amidei 2022" event in Gorizia, Italy.
